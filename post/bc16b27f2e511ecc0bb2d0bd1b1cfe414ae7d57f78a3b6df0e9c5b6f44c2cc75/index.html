Game of Thrones fan favorite Bella Ramsey will star as Ellie in HBO’s The Last of Us

HBO’s The Last of Us adaptation is set to feature a familiar face: Bella Ramsey, the actor who played the young ruler Lyanna Mormont on Game of Thrones, will star as Ellie in the upcoming TV series, according to The Hollywood Reporter. Hours after we initially published this post, it was confirmed that The Mandalorian star Pedro Pascal will play the role of Joel.

The prestige TV take on Naughty Dog’s The Last of Us game has been in the works for a while and has already accrued some impressive talent, with Chernobyl’s Craig Mazon attached as a writer and Kantemir Balagov, the director of the indie WWII drama Beanpole, directing the pilot.
