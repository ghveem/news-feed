The Persona soundtracks are coming to Spotify tomorrow at midnight

Persona-heads rejoice: it looks like all the original soundtracks for most of the games are coming to Spotify tomorrow at midnight. That’s right. The news comes courtesy of AniPlaylist, purveyors of fine anime song playlists.

At midnight, on January 5th...

Persona [Original Soundtracks & more] will be available on @Spotify ❤️

Playlist with all albums: https://t.co/lwIU3If6Gk pic.twitter.com/N4YyBvoDuo

— AniPlaylist (@AniPlaylist) January 4, 2021

According to their Twitter account, the music includes main game soundtracks from Persona 5, Persona 4 and Persona 4 Golden, Persona 3 and Persona 3 FES, and Persona 2. There’s also a bunch more stuff, apparently.

- Persona 5 OST
- P4 & Golden OST
- P3 & FES OST
