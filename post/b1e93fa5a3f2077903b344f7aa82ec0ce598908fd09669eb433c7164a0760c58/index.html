Judge orders Tim Cook and Craig Federighi to testify in Epic’s Fortnite case

From The Verge

The court order, spotted on Thursday by iMore and Apple Insider, gives us our first glimpse into how Apple’s executive leadership may be involved in the ongoing antitrust lawsuit, which is scheduled to go to trial as early as July of next year.
